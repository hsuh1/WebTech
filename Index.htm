My Hobbies

My favorite hobbies are League of Legends and Hearthstone. League of Legends, more commonly known as the LOL, is a Real-time strategy (RTS) game with Role Playing Game (RPG) aspects. I enjoy this game because of two main reasons. First, it is a game that requires quick reaction and great intuition. Second is that I get to enjoy both the strategy and RPG aspects of a game. There is a reason why the e-Sports is booming nowadays.Computer games are not simply games that you sit around and simply play anymore. You need to strategize, be able to work as a team, and have quick reactions. I enjoy Hearthstone because it is a quick card game. However, it has great variety of interesting cards to keep me interested. I play this game more often than I do LOL nowadays, because I could simply enjoy Hearthstone for about 20 minutes. While a game of LOL usually take around 40-50 minutes.
