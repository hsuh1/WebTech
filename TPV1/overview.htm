Overview of Social Entrepreneurship

What is Social Entrepreneurship?

There are many variations for the definition of the term social entrepreneurship. The definition that aligns with my own values the most is stated by the Center for the Advancement of Social Entrepreneurship (CASE). Accordinng to CASE, social entrepreneurship has two major aspects. The fist is "Recognizing and resourcefully pursuing opportunities to create social value" The second is "crafting innovative approaches to addressing critical social needs". So, social entrepreneurship is essentially the process of recognizing a social need and addressing the issue entrepreneurially to create social value.
